Ableton Live For Beginners

Learn Ableton Live the easy way

Ableton Live For Beginners gives students a thorough grounding in essential music production skills in Ableton Live. If you are serious about becoming a professional producer or sound engineer, then it is crucial to master these skills before you can progress further. It is perfect for beginners but even if you have been making music for some time you will benefit from learning professional techniques from an experienced producer with major artist credits.

You will learn to make music in Ableton Live by following along with our tutors and making a complete track from start to finish. In doing so, you will learn every technique you will need to begin your journey into music production, in any genre you like.

Read the course overview below for a more in-depth look at exactly what you can expect to learn in our Ableton Live Beginners Course

Remove The Struggle – Start Your Music Making Journey With Ableton LIVE Today!

Overview

Ableton LIVE is an incredibly powerful DAW, capable of the highest level of professional music production. In order to learn it, you need a tutorial with the same level of professionalism, and a teacher who is passionate about helping you become a better producer.

Everything you are taught has the sole purpose of giving you the best learning experience and the greatest transfer of knowledge possible. Simple, powerful & fun.

Course Content

You’ll get started in this Ableton Live tutorial in the easiest way possible. Making a basic beat in the first lesson and adding to it as the tutorial progresses with various clips and grooves.

You will go on to cover all aspects of making a track, from creating chords and melodies to using effects and instruments. Finally we will give you a breakdown of how to structure your song and mixing techniques for the final product.

How it works

The video lessons are available to stream from our website at any time and you can rewatch the content as many times as you need.

The course is split into three modules and their is a project submisssion at the end of each of them. One of our tutors will review your work and provide you with invaluable feedback on any areas you need to improve or focus on.
