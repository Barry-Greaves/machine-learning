Wavetable Masterclass

Journey into Wavetable Synthesis

Wavetable is the latest synth addition to the Ableton Live Suite. It rounds out the synth devices nicely, bringing a native wavetable synth directly to Live users.

This is new and seemingly limitless form of synthesis which can feel daunting to newcomers, particularly those who only have experience in traditional subtractive synthesis. We aim to demystify this powerful synth which comes as standard with Ableton Live Suite.

This course provides a thorough walkthrough of all Wavetable's features and functions. After completion, you will be well prepared to start designing new and inventive sounds. As an added bonus, it also includes 4 bonus sound tutorials loaded with practical tips and tricks to start applying your new skills straight away!

Learn how to design mind-blowing sounds with Ableton Wavetable
