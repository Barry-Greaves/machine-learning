Hardware Course

Combining Machines

Learn to combine machines in the studio and out live

With the wide range of inspiring and innovative gear available today, it can be hard to know where to start and machines will work well together. In this course, you learn to reach new sonic possibilities by combining the power of two or more machines.

We will start by guiding you through the minefield of possibilities and help you choose the equipment that is right for you and your sound. Once that is done all there is left to do is make music.

Mix your favourite synths to create your own original sound

Overview

We will cover all aspects of hardware perfmormance. From the studio to taking the gear to clubs and events on the road. We explain what machines will work together and what will best suit you depending on your needs as a perfomer and producer.

Once the set up has been decided we will run through how to get the machines singing together. Covering all aspects of hardware production this course will prepare you to ditch the computer and mouse and return to creating music with your ears and hands.

Course Content

From mono synths to poly synths, digital, analog, fm .... there is a huge range of equipment to choose from. We will guide you through this process and show you how to get it all working together.

You will learn how to control your hardware from pattern chaining to triggering programme changes, automating parameters and live mixing. You will learn how to make studio grade tracks without having to go new a DAW!

How it works

The video lessons are available to stream from our website at any time and you can rewatch the content as many times as you need.

The course is split into three modules and their is a project submisssion at the end of each of them. One of our tutors will review your work and provide you with invaluable feedback on any areas you need to improve or focus on.
