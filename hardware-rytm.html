Analog Rytm Masterclass

Learn to create your own unique rhythm

The ultimate analog/digital hybrid drum machine just got better. Thanks to its distinctive analog sound and its new sampling capabilities, the Analog Rytm MKII now truly offers limitless sound design possibilities.

We start this course with a detailed overview of Analog Rytm's data structure. This is where you learn about projects, kits, patterns, and how everything is organized. He then demonstrates how to start from scratch with a blank template, and how to create, save and load your own projects and kits.

Join us in this in depth course, and get ready to harness this powerful rhythm maker!

Learn all about this powerful groovebox

Overview

Over the duration of this masterclass we will touch on every aspect of the analog rytm. Upon completion you will understand how every aspect of the Rytm works and how to implement them into your own original tracks.

You will learn the best workflows and strategies to get the most of your Analog Rytm, such as using P-Locks, Retrigs, micro timing, Copy/Paste techniques, how to tweak your song live with Scene and Performance modes, and a lot more.

Course Content

We will dive straight into every major feature of the Analog Rytm starting with sampling!

After recording different sounds, we will demonstrates how to get creative with sound design, and how to record patterns by playing the pads live, or by programming Elektron's infamous step sequencer. Next thing you know, you'll have your first beat blasting!

How it works

The video lessons are available to stream from our website at any time and you can rewatch the content as many times as you need.

The course is split into three modules and their is a project submisssion at the end of each of them. One of our tutors will review your work and provide you with invaluable feedback on any areas you need to improve or focus on.
