About

Machine Learning - Electronic Music Academy

Machine Learning was founded in 2022. From our Dublin studio we have created an extensive library electronic music production tutorials and courses.

Our content is exclusively online with a mixture of paid and free options. Our courses range from focused workshops on specific pieces of hardware to general topics like mixing and mastering in Ableton Live.

We offer courses for every skill level, from novices just starting on their journey to intermediate and advanced producers looking to learn new skills and further refine their sound.
